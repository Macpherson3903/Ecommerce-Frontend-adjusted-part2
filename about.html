Our Story

Sourceway Integrated Services (SWISS) has been proudly serving Rivers State, since 2006. We started with one simple goal: to make top notch quality tools and industrial machines more affordable and easier to access. Founded by Mr. Ifeanyi Collins Anyanwu, SWISS has grown into a trusted distributor of iNGCO Tools Nationwide.

As a distributor of INGCO—a leading global brand known for reliable, high-performance tools—we supply a wide range of products for both home and professional use. From power tools to hand tools, we offer the same top quality INGCO is known for, at prices that help our customers get more done without overspending.

We believe that with the right tools, people can do great things. That’s why we focus on offering products that last, prices that are affordable, and service you can count on.
