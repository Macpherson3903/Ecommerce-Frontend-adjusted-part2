FAQs & Help

Find answers to your questions about our engineering tools and services.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy for tools?

Learn about our 30-day return policy.

You can return tools within 30 days of purchase with a receipt, provided they are unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team for assistance.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times for Nigeria and international orders.

Shipping within Rivers State takes 1-2 business days. Out of Rivers State shipping varies by location, typically 2-4 business days.

Are your tools compatible with international standards?

Information on tool compatibility.

Yes, our tools comply with international engineering standards such as ISO and DIN. Check product specifications for details.

Who pays for return shipping?

Details on return shipping costs.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, except for defective or incorrect items, where we provide a prepaid shipping label.

Do you ship to my country?

International shipping availability.

We ship to most countries worldwide. Enter your address at checkout to confirm availability and shipping costs.

Are replacement parts available for your tools?

Availability of replacement parts.

Yes, we offer replacement parts for many tools. Contact our support team with the product model number to check availability.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refund processing timeline.

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

What are the shipping costs?

Understand shipping fees.

Shipping costs vary based on order weight, destination, and shipping method. View the total at checkout before confirming your order.

Do you sell tools for specific industries?

Industry-specific tool offerings.

Yes, we offer tools for industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction. Browse our catalog or contact us for recommendations.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Learn about our exchange policy.

Exchanges are available for eligible items. Contact support@swisstools.store to initiate an exchange within 7 days.

What happens if my package is lost?

Handling lost shipments.

If your package is lost, contact us within 30 days of shipment. We’ll work with the courier to resolve the issue or provide a refund/replacement.

How do I contact customer support?

Reach our support team.

Contact us at support@swisstools.store or call +234 913 232 8654 . Our team is available Monday-Friday, 9 AM-5 PM WAT.

Can I place a bulk order for my business?

Bulk order information.

Yes, we offer bulk discounts for business orders. Email support@swisstools.store with your requirements for a quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

Available payment options.

We accept major credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.
